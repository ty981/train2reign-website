Account & Data Deletion

Your Data.
Your Choice.

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data from Train2Reign at any time. This page explains what we store, what gets deleted, and how to request deletion.

🗄️
What Data We Store
Information collected during your use of Train2Reign
Account
Email address, display name, authentication credentials
Profile
Height, weight, age, fitness goals, onboarding preferences
Workouts
Logged exercises, sets, reps, weights, workout history
Progress Photos
Progress photos you upload, associated dates and pose metadata
Biometrics
Body weight logs, measurements, and check-in data
Nutrition
Meal logs, calorie and macro tracking entries
Device & Usage
Device type, OS version, crash reports, and app usage analytics
Notifications
Push notification token and preference settings
🗑️
What Gets Deleted
Everything associated with your account is permanently removed
✓
Your account — email address, password hash, and authentication tokens
✓
All profile data — name, body stats, goals, and onboarding information
✓
Workout history — every logged session, exercise, set, and personal record
✓
Progress photos — all uploaded images and their metadata from cloud storage
✓
Biometric & nutrition logs — all weight entries, measurements, and meal records
✓
Notification tokens — push notification identifiers and preferences
✓
Usage analytics — any behavioral or crash data linked to your account
📱
Delete Your Account In-App
The fastest way to request deletion — available directly in the app
1
Open Train2Reign
Launch the app and make sure you are signed in to the account you wish to delete.
2
Go to Profile
Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar.
Bottom Nav → Profile
3
Open Settings
Tap the Settings icon in the top-right corner of the Profile screen.
Profile → Settings ⚙️
4
Select "Delete Account"
Scroll to the bottom of Settings and tap Delete Account. You will be asked to confirm before anything is deleted.
Settings → Delete Account
5
Confirm Deletion
Read the confirmation prompt and tap Delete permanently. Your account and all data will be queued for deletion immediately.
⚠️
Deletion is Permanent and Cannot Be Undone
Once you request account deletion, all your data — including workout history, progress photos, biometric logs, and profile information — is permanently erased from our servers. This action cannot be reversed and there is no grace period recovery option. Make sure you export or note any data you wish to keep before proceeding.

Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to delete your account in-app, or prefer to submit a written request, email us directly. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation once complete.
